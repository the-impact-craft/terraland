    About TerraLand

    TerraLand is a powerful and intuitive visual Terraform UI designed to simplify and streamline Terraform management for developers, DevOps teams, and infrastructure engineers. With its sleek, minimalistic design and real-time validation, TerraLand enhances your workflow by providing an organized, user-friendly interface that makes working with Terraform environments easier, faster, and more accessible.

    Whether you're managing complex infrastructure, reviewing Terraform plans, or executing operations, TerraLand allows you to interact with your infrastructure seamlessly and efficiently. From managing multiple environments to ensuring smooth Terraform execution, TerraLand empowers users with enhanced visibility and control over their infrastructure as code.

    TerraLand helps you take full advantage of Terraform’s capabilities, making it the ideal solution for both seasoned professionals and those just starting their infrastructure-as-code journey. With real-time validation, streamlined error handling, and an intuitive interface, TerraLand boosts your productivity while minimizing the complexity often associated with Terraform management.

    Features

    🚀 Workspace Management

    Effortlessly switch between multiple Terraform workspaces.

    📁 Project Explorer

    Visualize and navigate your Terraform files and directory structure.

    ⚡ Real-time Validation

    Execute Terraform commands with immediate feedback.

    ⏳ Command History

    View and reuse previously executed Terraform commands.

    🎨 Minimalist UI

    A clean, distraction-free interface built with the Textual framework.
